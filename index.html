Introduction

Formed out of a shared love for late seventies heyday Punk & New Wave music, THE XZITZ have a combined age of 235, with over a century of playing/gigging experience between them. Their unique selling point is their adherence to a narrow window of music from one of the most exciting periods of music in the 20th century. THE XZITZ ethos is simple: “If it was on ‘Top of The Pops’, we probably play it” – there are also a few curve ball tunes, thrown in for good measure and herein lies the appeal of this inimitable covers band. Despite the advanced years of the band, their desire to share the exciting, energetic music of their youth with audiences around the South of England, has led to them being regularly booked to play to audiences eager to participate in sing-along nostalgia. With over two hours of songs by the artists of the genre (Sex Pistols, The Damned, Ramones, The Undertones, Buzzcocks, The Skids, Sham 69, Dead Kennedys, Department S, etc.), there’s something for everyone.
